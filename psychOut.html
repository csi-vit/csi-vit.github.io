Psych Out
19th - 23rd March 2021
Psych Out, organized by CSI-VIT, is a 4-day annual puzzle competition which is completely based on Logical Reasoning only !

The study of logic is essential for computer science students, but no prior Computer Science Knowledge is required for this competition.

There shall be a live walkthrough session of the questions after the competition ends along with that the names of the winners will be declared. This competition will help the students to improve their general knowledge, cognitive skills, memory and concentration.
